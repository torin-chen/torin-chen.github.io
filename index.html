Research

A focused laser heats a sample; a second laser reads the surface temperature through thermoreflectance. From signals spanning 1 Hz–10 MHz I extract thermal conductivity, heat capacity, and interfacial conductance of micro/nanoscale structures.

Optical instrumentation

Designed and built a multi-technique pump–probe platform from the optical table up — laser optics, lock-in detection, automated acquisition.

Modeling & inverse problems

Multilayer heat-conduction models, sensitivity and SVD-based identifiability analysis — knowing what an experiment can and cannot measure.

Materials & applications

Thin films, liquids and solid–liquid interfaces, 3D anisotropic conductivity tensors, thermal interface materials under pressure, GaN electronics.
